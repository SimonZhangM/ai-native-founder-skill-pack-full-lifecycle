## Opening

Thank you for speaking with me. I am trying to understand how people handle [problem area]. I am not here to pitch a product. I am trying to learn what actually happens in your workflow.

## Past-behavior questions

1. Tell me about the last time you dealt with [problem].
2. What triggered it?
3. Who else was involved?
4. What did you do first?
5. What tools or workarounds did you use?
6. Where did the process slow down or break?
7. What was the cost in time, money, risk, or frustration?
8. What happens if this problem is not solved?
9. Have you tried to solve it before? What happened?
10. What would need to change for this to become a priority?

## Follow-up probes

- Can you walk me through that step by step?
- What did you do next?
- How often does that happen?
- Who owns the budget for solving this?
- What would make the current workaround unacceptable?
- What would make you stop caring about this?

## Avoid

- Would you use this?
- Would you pay for this?
- Do you like my idea?
- Would this be useful someday?

## Post-interview debrief

- What confirmed the hypothesis?
- What challenged it?
- What surprised us?
- What exact language did the user use?
- Did this persona feel close to the problem?
- Should we interview more of this segment or change target?
